History of AirPods and Apple Inc.

Apple Inc., founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, is a global leader in consumer electronics, software, and digital services. Headquartered in Cupertino, California, Apple revolutionized multiple industries with innovations like the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad.

  2. 2019: AirPods (2nd Generation) Introduced the H1 chip, enabling faster device switching, lower latency, and "Hey Siri" voice activation. Released with an optional wireless charging case.
  3. 2019: AirPods Pro Added Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) and Transparency Mode. Included in-ear tips for better fit and comfort. Improved water and sweat resistance, making them popular for workouts.
  4. 2021: AirPods (3rd Generation) Redesigned to resemble AirPods Pro but without ANC. Supported Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking. Extended battery life and improved sound quality.
  5. 2022: AirPods Pro (2nd Generation) Powered by the new H2 chip, offering smarter ANC and better audio quality. Enhanced battery life, case tracking via Find My, and a built-in speaker on the case.
